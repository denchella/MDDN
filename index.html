Infrastructure of Wellington

There's a huge variety of infrastructures in our compact city. However in our everyday lives, we often overlook these as just a way of life. Modern infrastructure comes in all different forms, with the influence of new technologies and ways of social interaction.

Bus Systems

Buses are a huge infrastructure in Wellington. As it is such a compact city this is a way for majority of commuters who live out of the city, to come in to the city everyday.

Bus Timetable

The city bus timetable is commonly seen at bus stops for an indication of bus travel times.

Snapper Card Top Up

This is a snapper card top up machine. Snapper cards are used by locals to ride the buses in Wellington.

Modern Transportations

With new technologies comes new and more efficient ways of transport. All though buses are a reliable constant for locals, new, fun and interesting ways of connecting us all over the city are emerging.

Onzo Bike

The Onzo, an electric bike, was the first addition to alternative quick transport within the city.

Flamingo Electric Scooter

Recently the addition of the Flamingo Electric Scooter has become the new and easy way to navigate around the city.

Advertising

Advertising isn't a new concept. However, in recent years advertising has become essential in business, art and social aspects of life. With the introduction of social media, using it to connect and interact with one another. Here in the city it is even taken to the streets.

Street Poster Board

Poster boards are seen all over the city, they are filled with events happening around the city to keep people informed and connected through these events.

Poster Wall

Similar to the Poster boards, they show events that are happening and advertise brands around the city.

Vic University Magazine

The univeristy magazine provides a connection between the students, sharing stories and information to relate over.
